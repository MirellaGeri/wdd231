ABOUT ME

My name is Geraldine Martinez Salcedo. I was born and raised in Lima, Peru.

My Journey

When I was a child, my dream was to become a pastry chef. Along the way, I developed some talents that I love, including drawing, crocheting, and making crafts.

I consider myself a versatile artist who enjoys working on all kinds of craft projects. My new dream is to become a graphic designer and create stories for my series.

When I'm not creating, I enjoy watching videos, taking walks with my sister, and spending time with family.
